# GradientBlock

A full-bleed panel filled with one of the brand gradients — the signature RB Marine look for heroes and section headers. Content is bottom-aligned; drop headings, taglines, or buttons inside.

- `gradient`: `hero`, `greenEnergy`, `lilacWave`, `powerSurge`.
- `size`: `sm` | `md` | `lg` | `full` (full-viewport, no radius).

```jsx
<GradientBlock gradient="hero" size="lg">
  <Display>Always on.</Display>
</GradientBlock>
```
